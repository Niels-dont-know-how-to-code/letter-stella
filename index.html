Stella,

I’ve been trying to figure out when exactly I knew. Maybe it was the first dance together, maybe it was the siesta in the park, maybe it was 4am when you were talking about societal standards and I realized I could listen to you forever.

What I know is this:

I love that I can be myself around you.

I love that we empower each other to be better.

I love your enthusiasm.

I love your high energy.

I love your zoomies.

I love that you are active and engaged.

I love that vegetables are breakfast food to you.

I love that you want to travel.

I love that you made me a crossword puzzle instead of just asking.

I love that you said “I don’t know yet” instead of lying to me.

I love that you challenged me to manage two full phrases in a conversation.

I love how the sun reflects in your eyes.

I love that you are the most stable person I have ever met, in the best possible way.

And I could go on for at least a hundred more.

I don’t need you to have a word for what this is yet.

I can see it in everything you do.

Take your time.

— Niels
